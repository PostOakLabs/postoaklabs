STAGE 6

Two P2P Channels

Transaction gossip and consensus messages never share a wire.

ETHWIRE (port 30303)
PurposeTransaction gossip
CarriesPending transactions, block bodies
RiskHigh-volume spikes during market activity
ACN — Autonity Consensus Network (port 20203)
PurposeTendermint BFT consensus messages only
CarriesProposals, prevotes, precommits, BLS quorum certificates
IntroducedAGC v0.13.0
RiskIsolated from transaction volume — consensus can proceed even during transaction-layer congestion
WHY TWO CHANNELS?
If consensus messages share ethwire bandwidth with transactions, a transaction surge can delay BFT messages and threaten liveness. ACN isolates consensus — both channels can be tuned independently.
AGC v1.1.1's clustering model (Originator / First Relayer / Local Relayer roles) was tested to 500 validators and 1,000 TPS in that configuration.
