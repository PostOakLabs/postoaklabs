02 Enforcement Timeline

EU Regulation 2024/1689 entered into force on 1 August 2024. Obligations activate in four waves.

2 Feb 2026 · LIVE
Article 5 Prohibitions
Real-time biometric identification in public spaces; social scoring by public authorities; subliminal manipulation. Prohibited practices now in force.
2 Aug 2026 · —
GPAI Obligations (Art. 53/55)
General-purpose AI model transparency, copyright compliance, training data summary. Systemic-risk models: adversarial testing, incident reporting.
2 Aug 2027 · 15 months
Annex III High-Risk (Art. 6)
Credit scoring, AML, insurance underwriting, biometric ID, HR screening in financial services. Full 9-obligation compliance framework, QMS, EU AI database registration.
2 Aug 2030 · 4+ years
Legacy General-Purpose AI
GPAI models placed on market before 2 Aug 2025 that are not significantly changed must comply by this extended deadline (Art. 113(4)).
