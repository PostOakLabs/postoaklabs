Market Context

BaaS Platform Fee Range

$0 – $25K+/month platform fees across providers, before per-account and per-transaction costs.

↑ 40–65% gross margins at 150K+ accounts
↓ Sub-10% margin below ~3K accounts

Interchange revenue share (the % of network interchange a BaaS fintech retains) is the primary gross margin lever. Providers offering 20–25% share typically offset higher per-account fees at medium scale.

📊 Avg debit interchange: ~1.5% of spend
📊 At 25K accts × $65 AOV × 18 tx: ~$440K gross interchange/mo

Implementation costs ($20K–$100K) amortise over 12–24 months — at Seed stage this dominates unit economics; by Scale it rounds to noise.

Sources: Nilson Report 2024; Andreessen Horowitz Fintech Index; KPMG BaaS Benchmark 2025.
