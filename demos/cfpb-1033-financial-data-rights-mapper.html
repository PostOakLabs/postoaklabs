Regulatory Context

CFPB §1033 Final Rule

Published October 2024 under Dodd-Frank §1033, this rule creates a US open banking framework requiring covered institutions to give consumers API-accessible data — displacing screen-scraping and legacy credential-sharing.

📅 First deadline: Apr 2026 (Tier 1)
📅 Final wave: Apr 2030 (TPPPs)

Financial Data Exchange (FDX) API is the industry-consensus technical standard. CFPB has not mandated FDX specifically, but rule-makers expect providers to use recognised standards for machine-readable delivery.

🔒 OAuth 2.0 + PKCE required for auth
🚫 Screen-scraping sunset at compliance date

Non-compliance risk: CFPB can bring enforcement under Dodd-Frank UDAAP authority — potential for 7-figure civil money penalties and consent orders.

⚠️ UDAAP enforcement risk post-deadline
