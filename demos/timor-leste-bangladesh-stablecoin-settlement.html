Value at Risk

        
Why this corridor is structurally hard

        
Timor-Leste runs on USD without a correspondent network of scale. Bangladesh runs BDT through BEFTN / bKash with capital controls and weak USD liquidity in-country. Together that is a 3-hop SWIFT route, 5–7 day finality, 4–6% all-in cost, and a 3-hour UTC overlap.

        
A USD-pegged stablecoin on a permissioned wholesale rail collapses the hops to one — provided collateral reserves, intraday liquidity, and the repo sweep are sized for the spike.

        
Legacy cost: 4.2–6.5% all-in

        
Modelled bridge: 0.45–0.85%

        
Refs: BIS Project Agorá, BIS Project Mariana, IMF AREAER 2024, World Bank Remittance Prices Q3 2024, IOSCO/CPMI principles for stablecoin arrangements (2022).
