Embedded finance M&A diligence

The single most common failure point in embedded-finance diligence is a target with revenue concentrated in a state or jurisdiction where it cannot legally operate without a licence it doesn't hold. The 50-state US MTL patchwork alone has tripped up at least four widely-reported BaaS targets in the last 24 months.

This navigator gives an acquirer (or a target preparing for diligence) a deterministic, side-by-side go / conditional / no-go view per market — and a control-gap list that maps to the FFIEC, FCA, and EU EMI/PI rulebooks.

MTL bond range: $0.5M–$7M / state
EMI initial capital: €350k
Refs: CSBS NMLS state MTL map 2024, FCA Handbook EMR/PSR, EU PSD2 / EMD2, FinCEN MSB regs (31 CFR 1010).
