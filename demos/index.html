FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is the Post Oak Labs Fintech Intelligence Showcase?

The Fintech Intelligence Showcase is a curated set of advisory scenarios and corridor case studies drawn from the Post Oak Labs practice and the AINumbers.co tool library. Every scenario runs entirely client-side, stores zero PII, exports Policy Mandate v1 mandates, and is discoverable over MCP.

Are the scenarios free, and do they store my data?

Yes. All scenarios are free and open-licensed under CC BY 4.0. They are 100% client-side: no data leaves your browser and no PII is collected or transmitted.

What is AP2 and why do the scenarios export it?

AP2 is Google’s Agent Payments Protocol (v0.2.0), a standard for expressing payment policies that AI agents can execute. Each scenario exports its result as a Policy Mandate (schema: @postoaklabs.com/policy-mandate-v1) so the output can be consumed by AP2-capable agents, replayed by an agent runtime, or reviewed by an audit team.

What is MCP and how do these tools use it?

MCP (Model Context Protocol) is an open standard for exposing tools to large language models. The underlying 400+ tool AINumbers catalog is MCP-native, so models such as Claude can call the tools directly and receive Policy Mandates in response.

Which regulatory regimes do the scenarios cover?

Across the catalog the scenarios cover DORA, NIS2, MiCA, the EU AI Act, CFPB §1033, FATF Recommendation 16 (Travel Rule), Basel III/IV, BIS 239, FCA DPC, ISO 20022 / CBPR+ (14 November 2026 structured-address deadline; MT/MX coexistence ended 22 Nov 2025), and the US GENIUS Act — each with verified enforcement-clock deadlines.

Who builds these scenarios and how do I go deeper?

They are built by Post Oak Labs, a firm specializing in tokenized payment infrastructure with advisory on production deployments across frontier markets. Explore the full AINumbers tool catalog at ainumbers.co, or browse by buyer archetype through the five hubs.
