FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is the Post Oak Labs Fintech Intelligence Showcase?

The Fintech Intelligence Showcase is a free, public catalog of 34 browser-based fintech demos drawn from the AINumbers.co tool library. Every demo runs entirely client-side, stores zero PII, exports AP2 v1.0 mandates, and is discoverable over MCP. All 34 are live as of May 2026.

Are the demos free, and do they store my data?

Yes. All 34 demos are free and open-licensed under CC BY 4.0. They are 100% client-side: no data leaves your browser and no PII is collected or transmitted.

What is AP2 and why do the demos export it?

AP2 (Agent Payments Protocol) v1.0 is a schema for expressing payment and policy mandates that AI agents can execute deterministically. Each demo can export its result as an AP2 v1.0 mandate, so the output can be replayed by an agent runtime or reviewed by an audit team.

What is MCP and how do these tools use it?

MCP (Model Context Protocol) is an open standard for exposing tools to large language models. The underlying 260-tool AINumbers catalog is MCP-native, so models such as Claude can call the tools directly and receive AP2 mandates in response.

Which regulatory regimes do the demos cover?

Across the catalog the demos cover DORA, NIS2, MiCA, the EU AI Act, CFPB §1033, FATF Recommendation 16 (Travel Rule), Basel III/IV, BIS 239, FCA DPC, ISO 20022 / CBPR+ (14 November 2026 SWIFT cutover), and the US GENIUS Act — each with verified enforcement-clock deadlines.

Who builds these demos and how do I go deeper?

They are built by Post Oak Labs, a firm specializing in tokenized payment infrastructure with production deployments in the Caribbean and South Asia. Explore the full 260-tool chain in the Tool Chain Composer, or browse by buyer archetype through the five hubs.
