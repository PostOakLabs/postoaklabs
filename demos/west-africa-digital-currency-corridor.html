Digital Currency Status by Jurisdiction

Nigeria
Live
eNaira (retail CBDC) · cNGN (regulated stablecoin)
eNaira launched Oct 2021, ~3.8M wallets. cNGN launched 2025 — CBN-backed naira stablecoin, interoperable with eNaira. AML: FINTRAC-equivalent CBN obligations apply.
ECCU
Live
DCash (retail CBDC) · 8 island nations
DCash launched Mar 2021 — first multi-jurisdiction CBDC. Issued by ECCB across Antigua & Barbuda, Dominica, Grenada, Montserrat, St Kitts, St Lucia, St Vincent, Anguilla. Cross-island PvP live.
Ghana
Paused
e-Cedi (retail CBDC pilot)
Pilot completed 2023; full launch paused pending Ghanaian cedi stabilisation. BoG reaffirmed commitment in Q1 2026. Interim: GhIPSS interbank rails used for digital settlement.
Senegal / WAEMU
Research
BCEAO digital CFA franc (eCFA — study phase)
BCEAO (Central Bank of West African States) studying a digital CFA franc for the 8-nation WAEMU zone. Wave and Orange Money dominate digital payments. No live CBDC as of 2026.
United Kingdom
Pilot 2026
Digital Pound (retail CBDC) — design phase
Bank of England & HMT in extended design phase. FCA stablecoin regulatory sandbox cohort live 2026. UK MTL required for corridor from Nigeria/Ghana. PSR APP fraud rules apply.
United States
No CBDC
GENIUS Act stablecoins (USD-pegged, regulated)
No US retail CBDC. GENIUS Act (Jul 2025) enables regulated USD payment stablecoins. Nigerian/West African remittance operators need FinCEN MSB registration + BSA/AML programme.
