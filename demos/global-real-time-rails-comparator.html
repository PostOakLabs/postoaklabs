Value at Risk

The "RTP problem"

Each rail is fast in its home jurisdiction and slow / expensive across borders. Wrong rail choice on a cross-border B2B corridor adds 3–5 days of finality risk and 2–4× the cost of a correctly-routed flow.

This comparator does the eligibility + scoring math up front so the routing decision is grounded in scheme rules — not an assumption that "instant means instant".

Cross-border B2B cost: 3.5–6.0%
