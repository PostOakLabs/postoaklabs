    Why SME credit needs signal layering

    Traditional SME underwriting uses two years of financials and a credit bureau pull. Open-finance signals — payment behaviour, daily deposit density, balance volatility — predict 12-month default ~2× better than bureau alone, especially for sub-$5M revenue businesses.

    This platform composes five tools so the underwrite, the working-capital sizing, and the facility recommendation come out of one deterministic flow — same model the buyer's portfolio manager would run.

    Bureau-only AUC: ~0.66
    + Open finance AUC: ~0.79
    Refs: BIS WP 1018, CFPB §1033 Open Banking RFC, Plaid SMB credit benchmarks 2024.
