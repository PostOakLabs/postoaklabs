    What a card programme actually costs

    Most issuer launches get the interchange uplift wrong by 30–80 bps because MCC mix sits outside CPS qualified rates, and PCI scope balloons after go-live when a forgotten POS tunnel pulls cardholder data into a previously out-of-scope segment.

    This studio computes both up front — the qualified-rate waterfall and the PCI scope expansion risk — so the unit-economics model in the BaaS / issuer-processor deck holds up after diligence.

    IC mis-qualified loss: 25–80 bps
    PCI scope creep cost: $0.5M–$3M / year
    Refs: Visa Public Pricing 2026, Mastercard Worldwide Interchange Rates 2026, PCI DSS v4.0.1, Reg II 12 CFR 235, EMVCo 3DS 2.3.
