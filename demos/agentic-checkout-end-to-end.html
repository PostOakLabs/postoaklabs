Value at Risk

Five protocol families landed on merchants in under two years

Google's AP2, OpenAI/Stripe's ACP, Coinbase's x402, Visa's Trusted Agent Protocol, and Mastercard Agent Pay each answer the same question differently: how does a merchant know an AI agent is allowed to pay? Pick wrong and you rebuild; ignore it and trusted agents look like bot traffic and get blocked at your own gateway.

This chain — discovery, mandate, settlement, signed audit — is the minimum production posture for accepting agent-initiated payments, whichever stack wins your category.

Discovery: A2A agent cards (v1.0)
Authority: AP2 verifiable credentials
Trust signal: RFC 9421 signatures
