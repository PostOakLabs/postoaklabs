Core Services

What Post Oak Labs builds and advises on

We build things and we advise on things, and we know the difference between the two. Most engagements start with an institution that needs one and discovers they need both.

Tokenized Payments

Tokenized A2A Cross-Border Payments

Design and integration of tokenized account-to-account payment rails supporting B2B and B2C cross-border transactions and remittances. We have advised on and supported production deployments across frontier markets. Market coverage and client profile →

Stablecoin

Stablecoin Strategy & Integration

Design and integration of stablecoin rails for commercial and central banks: issuance architecture, reserve management structure, and regulatory positioning. We advise on fit-for-purpose stablecoin approaches based on institutional context and jurisdiction.

CBDC

CBDC Integration & Architecture

Advisory on central bank digital currency design and deployment, covering interoperability with existing payment systems, integration with private sector infrastructure, and the structural questions that matter most to central bank teams evaluating CBDC.

Tokenized MMF

Tokenized Money Market Funds

Architecture and advisory for tokenized money market fund products. We can help commercial banks and corporates build and deploy standardized tokenized MMFs denominated in local currency, not just USD. Tokenized USD MMFs already exist; the gap is in local-currency equivalents for non-dollarized economies, where commercial banks can offer this access to corporate clients as a differentiated product.

Institutional Blockchain Advisory

Target Markets

Where we focus

We concentrate on markets where tokenized payment infrastructure is immature or absent, institutional demand is present, and the regulatory environment is moving in the right direction.

Primary · Highest Near-Term Opportunity

Latin America

Central and South America present the clearest near-term opportunity: high remittance volumes, regulatory openness in key markets, and commercial banking sectors looking to modernize cross-border payment infrastructure.

High Growth · Regulatory Momentum

Africa

Africa's mobile money penetration and regulatory experimentation with CBDC make it a significant opportunity for tokenized payment infrastructure, particularly for corridors that are currently expensive and slow.

Advisory · Production Deployments

Caribbean

We have advised on production deployments in the Caribbean region. Our familiarity with the regulatory environment, correspondent banking constraints, and the specific remittance dynamics of this region is direct.

Advisory · Production Deployments

South Asia

South Asia is a key focus region for tokenized payment infrastructure advisory. The remittance volumes, regulatory structure, and commercial banking sector in this region present ongoing opportunity as tokenized infrastructure matures.
